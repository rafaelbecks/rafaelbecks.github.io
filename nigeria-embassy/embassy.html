ABOUT NIGERIA

THE EMBASSY

The Embassy of the Federal Republic of Nigeria In Caracas, Venezuela was opened in 19XX, which is located at Calle Chivacoa cruce con Calle Tana, Qta. Leticia, San Roman, Caracas. The embassy is headed by an Ambassador, and has other Diplomatic staff and supporting staff. It provides Consular, Immigration and other services to Nigerians in Venezuela, and other nationals. The Embassy is also engaged in representational duties on behalf of the Government of the Federal Republic of Nigeria on political, economic, social educational, humanitarian, cultural and other global issues induding promoting trade ties and supporting new avenues for economic partnership between Venezuela and Nigeria.
