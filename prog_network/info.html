Graph search for prog music



The Project


ProgToNode is a new way to explore the world of progressive music. With ProgToNode you'll be able to search any artist, band or project of progressive rock/metal and discover the relationships between them.

The cool thing is that this relationships are represented in a visual way implementing graphs theory.

Yes, we put together prog music and math. Isn't that cool?

Why ProgToNode

We are building a graphic encyclopedia of progressive music

It's Easy


Read lines and nodes connecting musicians and bands is a cool way of learning and discover new music. The posiblities are endless.

It's Fun


You can spend hours connecting artists, you can even get surprised of where you get.

It's Experimental


The progressive music is about experimenting and reach new unknow horizons for music. Implementing mathematical methods, graphs theory and the hypotesis of six degrees of separation, We want to achieve to expand the horizons of discover.
